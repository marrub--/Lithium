== \#TUT_Movement_NAM | Movement
@@ \#TUT_Movement_DSC
- HORIZONTAL MOVEMENT

Each class has a <unique horizontal movement mechanic> bound to
{+speed} (Dodge / Run.) Some can <sprint>, <dodge>, or even <slide>.

- JUMPING

Press {+jump} to <jump> in supported maps. Some classes can
<double-jump> or <fly> by jumping.

- CROUCHING

Hold {+crouch} to <crouch> in supported maps. Some classes can quickly
<fall> by crouching.

== \#TUT_Enemies_NAM | Enemies 1
@@ \#TUT_Enemies_DSC
- MONSTERS

Lithium comes with <a set of new behaviours for monsters>, with the
option of <more difficult behaviour>. Several <monster mods also work>
with Lithium; however, if enemies don't have levels, they aren't
compatible.

! Please report broken monster mods.

- PHANTOMS

\c[Lith_Dark]Phantoms\c- will show up occasionally based on your
progression. They are very dangerous mid-bosses that give <unique
rewards>.

- BOSSES

<Boss arenas> will usually have unique behaviour, making them much
more difficult. The <Icon of Sin> has fully redone behaviour.

== \#TUT_EnemyMechanics_NAM | Enemies 2
@@ \#TUT_EnemyMechanics_DSC
- LEVELS

All enemies have a <level> and <rank>. Enemies with higher <levels>
will have <more health>, <deal more damage>, and <resist damage
better>. Enemies with higher <ranks> will have <higher level scaling>,
<more resistances>, and <gain experience faster>.

- DAMAGE TYPES

There are 8 damage types enemies can inflict:

@@
* Bullet Damage
* Electric Damage
* Fire Damage
* Fire Magic Damage
* Ice Damage
* Magic Damage
* Melee Damage
* Shrapnel Damage
@@

== \#TUT_Items_NAM | Items
@@ \#TUT_Items_DSC
- SHOPPING

Upgrades can be acquired by buying them with \cnScore\c-. Some classes
also have a separate shop where weapons can be bought.

! Some classes do not have upgrades yet.

- PERMANENT UPGRADES

\c[Lith_Dark]Phantoms\c- will drop a set of six or more <permanent
upgrades> upon defeat depending on the class you chose. These
<permanent upgrades> may be necessary to buy certain <upgrades> or
unlock new mechanics.

- POWERUPS

@@
* The <Barrier> power-up grants partial invulnerability by deflecting many attacks.
* The <Mega-charge> will give you <magic armor> that protects you from damage.
@@

== \#TUT_Story_NAM | Story
@@ \#TUT_Story_DSC
! This applies only to Lithium's maps.

- TERMINALS

Walk up to a terminal and press {+use} to <read it>.

Some terminals may be <necessary to progress>.

- DIALOGUE

Some people and things are not enemies, at least not right now.

Walk up to friendlies and press {+use} to <talk> with them.

Dialogues are <not necessary to progress>.

== \#TUT_MarineMove_NAM | Marine 1
@@ \#TUT_MarineMove_DSC
- SLIDING

Press or hold {+speed} (Dodge / Run) while on the ground or in the air
to <slide horizontally>. You will be moved in whatever direction
you're actively moving in.

- JUMPING

Press {+jump} (jump):

@@
* On the ground to <jump> normally.
* While in the air to <double-jump>.
* While sliding to <super-jump>.
* After <double-jumping> or <super-jumping> to activate <flight>.
@@

== \#TUT_MarineItem_NAM | Marine 2
%% \#TUT_MarineItem_DSC
- WEAPONS

* Most of \c[Lith_Green]Marine\c-'s weapons \cddo have\c- <alt-fires> ({+altattack}.)
* No weapons use the <Weapon Action> ({+user1}) button.
* The <Special Action> ({+lith_k_qact}) button will quickly attack with a knife.

- ITEMS

\c[Lith_Green]Marine\c-'s \c[Lith_Dark]Phantoms\c- will drop:

* Devices needed to buy more kinds of upgrade.

== \#TUT_CyberMageItem_NAM | Cyber-Mage 2
%% \#TUT_CyberMageItem_DSC
- WEAPONS

* Most of \c[Lith_Red]Cyber-Mage\c-'s weapons \ckdo not\c- have <alt-fires> ({+altattack}.)
* The Sub-Machine Gun uses the <Weapon Action> ({+user1}) button to manually reload the chamber.

- ITEMS

\c[Lith_Red]Cyber-Mage\c-'s \c[Lith_Dark]Phantoms\c- will drop:

* Spells you can cast with <mana>.

== \#TUT_DarkLordMove_NAM | Dark Lord 1
@@ \#TUT_DarkLordMove_DSC
- SPRINTING

Hold {+speed} (Dodge / Run) while on the ground to <sprint>. Your
movement speed will be greatly increased, but you won't be able to use
<weapons> except for your <sub-weapons>.

- MOBILITY

\c[Lith_Blue]Dark Lord\c- has limited mobility, but can still move
around maps decently well. Recoil can turn the tides of combat.

== \#TUT_DarkLordItem_NAM | Dark Lord 2
%% \#TUT_DarkLordItem_DSC
- WEAPONS

* \c[Lith_Blue]Dark Lord\c-'s <alt-fire> button ({+altattack}) is bound to activating <sub-weapons>.
* <Sub-weapons> are infinite use, charge quickly, and can be <used at any time>.
* The <Special Action> ({+lith_k_qact}) button will switch the equipped <sub-weapon>.
* The <Minigun> uses the <reload> button ({+reload}) to wind up without firing.
* <Ruzuk> uses the <Weapon Action> ({+user1}) button to aim down sights.

- ITEMS

\c[Lith_Blue]Dark Lord\c-'s \c[Lith_Dark]Phantoms\c- will drop:

* Three additional <sub-weapons>.
* One additional <weapon>.
* A regenerating <shield> that will protect you from taking damage.
* A device needed to buy certain upgrades.
